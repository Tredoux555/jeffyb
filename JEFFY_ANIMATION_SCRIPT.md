# JEFFY'S FREE PRODUCT PROGRAM
## Animation Script v1.0

**Duration:** ~90-120 seconds
**Style:** 2D Character Animation (friendly, modern, colorful)
**Tone:** Fun, friendly, trustworthy

---

## CHARACTER DESIGNS

### JEFFY (Main Character)
- **Species:** Friendly mascot creature (think Duolingo owl meets delivery mascot)
- **Body:** Round/oval, bright JEFFY YELLOW (#FCD34D)
- **Size:** Short and cute, approachable
- **Face:** Big friendly eyes (white with black pupils), small happy mouth
- **Accessories:** Small delivery cap (dark navy blue), optional tiny delivery bag
- **Arms/Hands:** Small stubby arms that can gesture expressively
- **Legs:** Short stubby legs or can float/bounce
- **Personality:** Enthusiastic, helpful, slightly mischievous but trustworthy
- **Voice:** Friendly, upbeat, slightly high-pitched but not annoying

### THABO (Friend Character)
- **Species:** Human
- **Appearance:** Young adult male, casual clothes (t-shirt, jeans)
- **Skin tone:** Medium brown
- **Hair:** Short black hair
- **Expression:** Curious, then excited
- **Voice:** Normal conversational tone, relatable

## NOTES FOR ANIMATOR

1. **Jeffy's personality:** Always upbeat, bouncy movements, exaggerated expressions
2. **Timing:** Leave slight pauses for dialogue to breathe
3. **Transitions:** Use playful wipes, bounces, and pops - nothing harsh
4. **The input box:** Should look like the ACTUAL UI on the website for continuity
5. **Spark scene:** Make it funny but safe-looking (Thabo's reaction is comedic, not scary)
6. **Approvals animation:** Should feel satisfying, like a game progress bar filling up
